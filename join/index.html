So you want to be RedFox member?

As a RedFox member, you'll have access to a range of exciting features and benefits and much more!

Interested in joining RedFox and becoming part of our vibrant community of gamers? Here's how you can join us and what you can expect as a RedFox member:

First, click on the link provided at the bottom of this page to join our Discord server. If you don't already have a Discord account, you'll need to sign up for one. Once you're on our server, click on the "Accept Invite" button to officially join RedFox.


Basic things you can do there:

Connect and chat with other members in our active and friendly community. Share tips, strategies, and experiences with like-minded gamers from all over the world.

Join or watch RedFox E-Sports events and tournaments. We host regular competitions and events across a range of games and genres, giving our members the chance to test their skills against some of the best gamers out there.

Experience a toxic-free community where you can enjoy playing games without fear of being attacked or harassed by other players. Our community is built on respect, sportsmanship, and fair play, and we take pride in maintaining a safe and inclusive environment for all our members

Get in-game benefits that will help you level up and achieve your gaming goals. We offer a range of rewards and bonuses that are exclusive to RedFox members, giving you an edge over your competition and helping you feel like a true winner.

Stay up to date with the latest news and events in our weekly community updates. We'll keep you informed about all the exciting things happening in the world of gaming, as well as any new developments or updates within the RedFox community

There's even a chance to win a VIP membership! As a new member of RedFox, you'll have a 15% chance of being selected for a VIP membership, which gives you access to even more exclusive benefits and perks.

We're constantly growing and evolving, and we're always looking for new ways to enhance the RedFox experience for our members. In the future, we're planning to launch a RedFox Develop program that will give members the chance to contribute to the community and help shape its future. Stay tuned for more updates on this exciting initiative!
